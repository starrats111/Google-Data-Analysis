What We Do

Affiliate-driven Google Ads operations — a complete loop from merchant partnerships to data-driven optimization.

01

Merchant Partnerships

We work with leading international affiliate networks to source and evaluate merchants and products worth promoting. Our team reviews product quality, landing page experience, commission structures, and settlement terms — only merchants that meet our standards enter our promotion pipeline.

  • Merchant product and landing page evaluation
  • Commission structure and partnership terms analysis
  • Long-term relationship management and performance tracking
02

Google Ads Campaign Operations

Advertising is our core capability. Our internal team runs Google Search and Performance Max campaigns under our own Google Ads Manager (MCC) account, driving purchase-intent traffic to partner merchants' official websites.

  • Keyword Research — High-intent commercial keywords across multiple languages and markets.
  • Ad Creative — Responsive Search Ads copywriting plus assets such as sitelinks and callouts.
  • Bidding & Budgets — Manual CPC, Target CPA, and Maximize Clicks strategies with daily budget discipline.
  • Targeting — Precise country, region, and language targeting.

All advertising budgets are paid by our company directly to Google. We do not manage campaigns for third-party clients, and all campaigns comply with the Google Ads Affiliate Program Policy.

03

Analytics & ROI Optimization

We synchronize campaign performance data (spend, clicks, impressions, conversions) and affiliate commission data daily, analyze the return on investment of every campaign in a unified internal dashboard, and reallocate keywords, bids, and budgets accordingly — cutting underperformers and scaling winners.

04

Content Marketing

Beyond paid advertising, we produce SEO content related to our partner merchants' products and publish it on our own content sites, generating sustained organic search traffic that complements paid search campaigns.

Business Boundary

Scope of Business

Wenzhou Fengdu Advertising & Media Co., Ltd. advertises exclusively for its own affiliate marketing business.

No Third-Party Ads

We do not provide ad agency, campaign management, or account hosting services to third parties. All campaigns serve our own affiliate business.

No Software Sales

We do not sell any software products or SaaS services. Our internal campaign management tool is used by our own employees only.

Full Compliance

All campaigns comply with the Google Ads Affiliate Program Policy and applicable advertising laws and regulations.
