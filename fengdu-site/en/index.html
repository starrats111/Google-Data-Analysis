About Fengdu

Who We Are

Founded in December 2025 and registered in Taishun County, Wenzhou, Zhejiang, our company focuses on affiliate marketing: we source partner merchants from international affiliate networks, and our internal advertising team runs Google Search and Performance Max campaigns that bring high purchase-intent users to merchants' websites, earning commissions on resulting sales.

We operate on an own-account, own-budget, in-house basis — every ad account belongs to our own Google Ads Manager (MCC) account, advertising costs are billed by Google directly to our company, and all campaigns comply with the Google Ads Affiliate Program Policy and applicable advertising regulations.

What We Do

Our Business

The full affiliate-marketing value chain — from merchant selection to campaign optimization — handled entirely in-house.

01

Merchant Partnerships

We work with leading international affiliate networks to source and evaluate merchants and products, reviewing product quality, landing-page experience, commission structures, and payment cycles to build long-term, performance-based partnerships.

02

Google Ads Campaigns

Our internal team runs Google Search and Performance Max campaigns under our own MCC — covering keyword research, bidding strategies, geographic and language targeting, Responsive Search Ad copywriting, and ad asset production, end to end.

03

Analytics & ROI Optimization

We track spend, clicks, conversions, and commission income daily, and continuously optimize campaign structure around return on investment — cutting under-performers and scaling winners with data-driven decisions.

04

Content Marketing

We produce SEO content related to partner merchants' products and publish it on our own content sites, bringing sustained organic search traffic that complements paid search campaigns.

Compliance
