Affiliate Marketing · Google Ads

Wenzhou Fengdu Advertising & Media Co., Ltd.

We are an affiliate marketing company based in Wenzhou, Zhejiang, China. We run Google Search and Performance Max campaigns to drive purchase-intent traffic to partner merchants' websites and earn commissions on resulting sales.

All campaigns are operated by our own internal advertising team under our own Google Ads Manager (MCC) account, with all advertising budgets paid by our company directly to Google. We do not manage campaigns on behalf of third-party clients.
