About Us

Wenzhou Fengdu Advertising & Media — founded December 2025 — affiliate marketing × Google Ads.

Company Overview

Wenzhou Fengdu Advertising & Media Co., Ltd. (温州丰度广告传媒有限公司) was founded on December 9, 2025 and is an affiliate marketing company based in Wenzhou, Zhejiang, China. Our business model is to drive purchase-intent traffic to partner merchants' websites through Google Search and Performance Max advertising, and we earn commissions on resulting sales.

All Google Ads campaigns are operated by our own internal advertising team under our own Google Ads Manager (MCC) account. All advertising budgets are paid for by our company directly to Google. We do not manage campaigns on behalf of third-party clients. All campaigns comply with the Google Ads Affiliate Program Policy.

Our Business Model

We are an affiliate marketing company. We source partner merchants from international affiliate networks. Our team evaluates merchant products, selects high-intent commercial keywords, and runs Google Search and Performance Max campaigns to drive purchase-intent traffic to those merchants' websites. We earn commissions on resulting sales or conversions.

To operate at scale across multiple international markets and languages, we developed an in-house campaign management tool deployed on our own infrastructure, used for campaign creation, budgeting, bidding, and performance reporting. The tool is used exclusively by our internal employees and is not offered as a service or product to any third party.

Team & Capabilities

  • Merchant Partnerships — Sourcing and evaluating partner merchants from international affiliate networks.
  • Campaign Operations — Full-lifecycle Google Search and Performance Max management: keyword research, bidding strategies (Manual CPC, Target CPA, Maximize Clicks), geographic and language targeting, Responsive Search Ads, and ad assets.
  • Budget Control — Real-time budget and bid management with strict cost discipline.
  • Performance Analytics — Daily tracking of spend, clicks, impressions, conversions, and commissions, with ROI-driven optimization.
  • Content Marketing — SEO content production that complements paid search with organic traffic.
