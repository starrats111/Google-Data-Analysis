Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Wenzhou Fengdu Advertising & Media Co., Ltd. ("we", "us") respects and protects the privacy of visitors to this website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit fengdu-ads.top (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

This Site is an informational corporate website. It has no user registration and does not actively collect personally identifiable information from visitors.

  • Information you provide — When you contact us by email or phone, we receive the name, contact details, and inquiry content you choose to share, used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Technical information — The Site is served through a content delivery network (CDN). The CDN provider may keep access logs (such as IP address, browser type, and access time) for security and performance purposes. We do not use these logs to identify individuals.

2. Cookies

The Site itself does not set tracking cookies. The CDN provider may set strictly necessary security cookies to keep the Site operating normally.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and business communications;
  • Maintain the security and normal operation of the Site.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect, including site-wide HTTPS encryption and access controls. Email correspondence is accessible only to relevant internal staff.

5. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (such as Google policy pages). The privacy practices of those websites are governed by their own policies, for which we are not responsible.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
