Sensitive-Skin Barrier-Friendly Starter Trio

A gentle cleanser, moisturiser and sunscreen that form a low-drama skincare backbone you can rely on.

For sensitive skin, the priority isn’t “maximum actives” but fewer mistakes. This trio strips out high-irritation ingredients, leaving mild surfactants, barrier-friendly hydration scaffolds, and a lower-irritation sunscreen.

01 Cleanser: only what’s necessary

Choose amino-acid or similarly mild surfactants; avoid harsh cleansing or built-in exfoliation claims. Easy foam, easy rinse, no squeaky feel — no need for that “stripped” clean.

02 Moisturiser: centred on barrier repair

Prioritise ceramides, cholesterol and fatty acids; switch texture seasonally between lotion and cream. If you’re sensitive to fragrance, colourants or alcohol, pick formulas without them or with very low levels.

03 Sunscreen: the everyday “invisible shield”

Rather than chasing the highest number, pick a texture you’ll actually wear daily. Mineral vs. hybrid filters each have pros/cons; focus on whether it pills, clashes with makeup, or feels comfortable.

Tip: if your barrier is clearly compromised (frequent stinging or flaking), simplify further under medical guidance and pause all actives.
