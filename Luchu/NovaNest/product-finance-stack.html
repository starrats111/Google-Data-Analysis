Calm Money Stack: Reference Tool & Coverage Layout

A simple, boring structure that keeps your everyday finance and insurance understandable.

This reference layout mirrors the article’s structure: one spending account, one savings hub, one brokerage, and a basic insurance layer. The exact institutions and products will vary by country, but the checklist remains useful.

01 Spending account

  • Primary checking / current account with debit card.
  • Low or transparent fees, strong online / mobile banking.
  • Instant notifications and exportable statements.

02 Savings hub

  • High‑yield savings or money‑market style account for emergency funds.
  • Government‑backed deposit protection where available.
  • No complex lock‑ups; clear rules for withdrawals.

03 Brokerage

  • Low‑cost access to broad index funds or ETFs.
  • Good reporting tools and tax documents for your jurisdiction.
  • No need for day‑trading or complex derivatives to execute a calm plan.

04 Insurance coverage

  • Health insurance that meaningfully limits catastrophic medical expenses.
  • Term life insurance if you have financial dependents.
  • Disability / income protection where available and affordable.

This page is a structural guide only. For concrete product names and numbers, please consult local regulations and a qualified advisor.
