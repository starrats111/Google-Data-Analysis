City Commute Capsule Wardrobe Checklist

A copy-paste commute checklist so a few pieces deliver steady, un-boring outfits.

The list follows the article’s scenario: city commute + light business. If your workplace is more formal or more casual, adjust a few items accordingly.

01 Tops (8 pieces)

  • Crisp white shirt ×2 (different collars).
  • Light blue shirt ×1.
  • Fine stripe shirt ×1.
  • Light knits ×2 (beige / charcoal).
  • Plain logo-free tees ×2 (white / black).

02 Bottoms (6 pieces)

  • Straight tailored trousers ×2 (black / charcoal).
  • Dark straight denim ×1.
  • Light khaki chino ×1.
  • Solid midi skirt ×1.
  • Drapey wide-leg trousers ×1.

03 Outerwear & Shoes (3 + 3)

  • Unstructured blazer ×1 (pairs with many bottoms).
  • Knee-length trench or long coat ×1.
  • Short jacket ×1 (denim or tech fabric).
  • Loafers ×1, lace-up dress shoes ×1, white sneakers ×1.

Buying tip: focus on fit, easy-care fabric and machine-washability before brand names.

For one-week outfit examples using these pieces, see: Capsule Wardrobe in Practice: 20 Pieces for a Season of Commuting.
