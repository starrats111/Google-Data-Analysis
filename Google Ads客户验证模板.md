# Google Ads 客户验证模板

> 适用场景：Google Ads `Verify your business operations` / Business Operations Verification（BOV）
> 
> 更新时间：2026-03-26
> 
> 用途：用于协助客户完成 Google Ads 客户验证，并沉淀为可复用参考模板。

---

## 一、先看结论：这类验证最容易卡在哪里

Google Ads 这类验证，不是简单填公司名和网址就能过。最容易被退回的点通常是：

1. **公司名称、付款主体、营业执照名称不一致**
2. **网站归属说不清楚**，比如广告账户投的是 A 网站，但资料填的是 B 网站
3. **业务关系链没讲清楚**，例如代运营、代理、联盟、品牌授权、履约方、供货方、域名持有人没有写明
4. **材料不合规**，比如上传截图、模糊文件、过期资料、网页截图代替证件
5. **把没有真实关系的第三方写进网站或表单**，但拿不出合同、授权书、发票、邮件等证明
6. **把事实字段写成猜测**，例如谁履约、谁提供产品、谁制作广告内容、谁登录 Ads 账户，不能乱写

所以核心原则只有一句：

**所有主体、网站、付款人、履约方、品牌方、广告操作方，必须前后一致，且能拿材料证明。**

---

## 二、正式填写前，先收集这些资料

### 2.1 基础资料

- Google Ads 客户 ID（Customer ID）
- 当前投放绑定的网站域名
- 公司/个人的法定名称
- 公司注册地址
- 邮编
- Google Ads 登录邮箱
- 付款主体名称
- 付款主体所在地

### 2.2 身份/主体证明材料

按主体类型准备：

#### 企业主体
- 营业执照 / 公司注册证明
- 如适用：品牌注册、经营许可证、行业资质、税务/主体证明
- 若网站使用品牌名或 DBA / Trade Name：材料中最好能体现该名称

#### 个人主体
- 政府签发身份证件
- 地址证明（如平台要求）

#### 未注册个体/工作室
- 身份证件
- 地址证明
- 能证明经营活动的补充材料

### 2.3 关系证明材料

如果涉及以下任一情况，建议提前准备证明文件：

- 代理商/代运营公司管理 Google Ads
- 外部公司制作广告内容
- 外部公司制作网站内容
- 网站域名归属人不是广告主
- 产品/服务由第三方履约
- 使用其他品牌、授权品牌、加盟品牌、分销品牌
- 联盟营销 / Lead Gen / Affiliate / 中间商模式

可用材料示例：

- 服务合同
- 品牌授权书
- 合作协议
- 发票
- 企业抬头说明函
- 往来邮件
- 订单/履约说明
- 能证明两家公司关系的正式文件

### 2.4 材料格式要求

优先使用：

- PDF
- JPG / JPEG
- PNG

注意：

- 不要上传网页截图代替证件
- 不要上传模糊照片
- 不要上传压缩包或网盘链接
- 若原件是彩色，尽量上传彩色版本
- 所有关键信息要清晰可见
- 可遮挡敏感信息，但不要遮掉主体名称、地址、日期、编号等关键字段

## 三、推荐填写流程

建议按下面顺序做，不要直接上来就提交：

1. 先确认 Ads 客户 ID、网站、付款主体
2. 再确认广告主主体是谁
3. 再确认产品/服务是谁提供、谁履约
4. 再确认是否有代理、联盟、代运营、内容制作方
5. 再检查网站上是否出现了其他品牌/公司名称
6. 最后再回到表单逐题填写
7. 提交前把所有名称逐字核对一遍

## 四、逐题填写模板

下面不是“唯一标准答案”，而是**填写骨架**。实际提交时必须根据客户真实情况改写。

---

## Part 1 - About the advertiser

### 1）Google Ads account customer ID

填写：

- 客户 ID，例如：`123-456-7890`

注意：

- 必须填当前登录账号下关联的真实 Ads 账户

### 2）Associated website

填写：

- 当前 Ads 账户实际投放使用的网站，例如：`https://example.com`

注意：

- 必须与广告落地页实际使用网站一致
- 不要填公司官网但广告实际投的是另一个站

### 3）Is the website listed in question 2 still used with this Ads account?

#### 若是
填：`Yes`

#### 若否
需要补充：
- 为什么不再使用
- 新的网站是什么
- 当前广告实际链接到哪个网站

模板：

```text
The previously listed website is no longer used for this Ads account.
The current website used in campaigns is https://example.com.
The old website was retired because [reason].
```

### 4）Who pays for the Google Ads account?

按真实情况选择：

- 公司付款
- 个人付款

#### 公司付款模板

```text
The Google Ads account is paid for by [Legal Company Name], which is the business advertised through this account.
```

#### 个人付款模板

```text
The Google Ads account is paid for by [Full Legal Name].
```

注意：

- 这里填写的付款主体，后续 disclosure、付款资料、主体证明最好一致
- 名称要和营业执照/证件完全一致

### 5）Is there another business that manages campaigns or campaign creation?

#### 没有外部公司代管
填：`No`

#### 有外部公司代管
填：`Yes`，并说明：
- 公司名称
- 网站
- 关系描述

模板：

```text
Business name: [Agency Name]
Website: https://agency-example.com
Relationship: [Agency Name] provides paid advertising management services for our Google Ads account, including campaign setup, optimization, and reporting, under a formal service agreement.
```

建议上传：
- 服务合同
- 发票
- 往来邮件
- 授权说明

## Part 2 - About you

### 6）Your name

填写当前实际负责本次验证的人名。

### 7）Your company name

填写你所在公司名称。

如果你是代理公司代客户处理，要按真实身份写，不要混淆“填写人公司”和“广告主公司”。

### 8）Street address
### 9）City
### 10）Zip / Postal Code
### 11）Email address you sign in to Google Ads with

按真实信息填写，确保：

- 地址和主体证明一致
- 邮箱确实能登录当前 Ads 账户

## Part 3 - About the advertiser’s business model and partnerships

### 12）Please describe the company's type of business.

#### 自营电商模板

```text
We are an e-commerce business selling [product category] directly to consumers through our website.
```

#### 品牌方/制造商模板

```text
We are a brand owner and seller of [product category], and we market and sell our products through our website.
```

#### 代理/中介/Lead Gen 模板

```text
We are a lead generation business. We promote [service category] online and connect interested customers with qualified service providers.
```

#### 联盟营销模板

```text
We are an affiliate marketing business. We promote products or services online and direct users to offers from our partner merchants.
```

### 13）Please describe the company's business model.

通用模板：

```text
Our business sells/promotes [products or services] through our website. Customers discover us through online advertising, visit our website, review our offerings, and then [place an order / submit an inquiry / contact us / get redirected to the provider]. Our target customers are [customer type].
```

如果是电商，可改成：

```text
We operate an online store selling [product category]. Customers browse products on our website, place orders online, and receive the goods through our fulfillment process.
```

如果是 lead gen / 中介，可改成：

```text
We collect inquiries from users interested in [service type] and pass qualified leads to the businesses that ultimately provide the service.
```

### 14）Who are your customers / target audience?

模板：

```text
Our target audience is [audience description], and our customers are [actual paying customers if different].
```

示例：

```text
Our target audience is adults interested in home fitness products, and our customers are consumers who purchase these products directly through our website.
```

### 15）How does your company interact with your target audience?

模板：

```text
We interact with our audience through our website, email, and customer support channels. Customers can browse our offerings, contact us with questions, and complete purchases or inquiries online.
```

### 16）Select the business type that best reflects the company

这里会根据你的业务模式出现分支问题，常见分支如下。

#### A. 自营 / 自有产品 / 自有网站
关键逻辑：
- 产品或服务由自己提供
- 履约主体是自己或自己控制的体系
- 网站内容和品牌归属清晰

#### B. 第三方履约 / 代发货 / 服务由他方提供
需要准备：
- 谁提供产品/服务
- 你和第三方什么关系
- 为什么你有权销售/推广
- 有无合同/授权

模板：

```text
The products/services promoted on the website are fulfilled by [Third Party Name]. We work with them under [distribution / fulfillment / service / reseller] arrangements.
```

#### C. Affiliate / 联盟模式
需要说明：
- 你不是最终履约方
- 你负责引流/推广
- 谁是最终商家/服务方
- 关系证明是什么

模板：

```text
We operate as an affiliate partner. We promote offers from [Merchant Name] and direct users to the merchant or collect leads in accordance with our affiliate relationship.
```

#### D. Brand authorization / Dealer / Franchise
需要说明：
- 品牌归谁所有
- 你基于什么授权使用品牌
- 是否有授权书、加盟协议、经销协议

模板：

```text
We are authorized to market and sell products under the [Brand Name] brand based on a formal authorization / dealer agreement.
```

### 17）Do you provide the products / services listed on the website?

#### 如果是自己提供
模板：

```text
Yes. Our company directly provides the products/services listed on the website.
```

#### 如果不是自己直接提供
模板：

```text
No. The products/services are provided by [Third Party Name], and our company works with them as [agency / reseller / affiliate / lead generation partner / distributor].
```

如果表单追问 “Who provides the products/services?”、“How are they provided?”，可继续填：

```text
The products/services are provided by [Third Party Name] through [fulfillment / direct service delivery / merchant checkout / partner network]. We are authorized to market these offerings under our business relationship.
```

### 18）Does the company provide paid marketing, paid advertising, or paid lead generation services?

这是高风险题。

#### 若不是代理/联盟/代获客业务
填：`No`

#### 若是代理、代投、Lead Gen、Affiliate
必须如实填：`Yes`

模板：

```text
Yes. Our company provides [paid advertising / marketing / lead generation] services. We promote [client/partner/merchant name] through this account based on our business relationship.
```

如果还有外部营销公司登录广告账户，也要补：

```text
[Company Name] signs in to the Google Ads account and helps manage ad operations under our agreement.
```

建议上传：
- 服务合同
- 授权书
- 发票
- 邮件证明

## Part 4 - Body Questions

### 19）Describe how the target audience receives the goods or services promoted in your ads.

#### 电商模板

```text
Customers click our ads, visit our website, place orders online, and receive the goods through our shipping and fulfillment process.
```

#### 线索收集模板

```text
Users click our ads and submit their information through our website. We then connect them with the relevant service provider, who delivers the service.
```

#### 联盟跳转模板

```text
Users click our ads, visit our website or landing page, and are then directed to the merchant or provider where they complete the purchase or receive the service.
```

### 20）Who creates ad content for this Ads account?

#### 自己制作

```text
Our in-house team creates the ad content, including ad copy, keywords, and targeting.
```

#### 代理公司制作

```text
[Agency Name] creates and manages ad content for this Ads account under our service agreement.
```

### 21）Who creates the content of your website?

#### 自己制作

```text
Our internal team creates and maintains the content on our website.
```

#### 外包制作

```text
[Vendor Name] helps create or maintain website content under our agreement, while we control and approve the final content.
```

### 22）Who would a customer hold responsible for non-fulfillment of goods or services advertised through Google?

这是另一个关键题，不能模糊。

#### 自己负责

```text
Customers would hold [Legal Company Name] responsible for fulfillment and any issues related to the goods or services promoted through Google Ads.
```

#### 第三方负责

```text
Customers would hold [Third Party Name] responsible for fulfillment, as they are the provider of the goods/services. Our company acts as [agency / reseller / affiliate / lead generation partner].
```

### 23）Do any other parties sign in to the Ads account?

#### 没有
填：`No`

#### 有
需写明：
- 公司名称
- 网站
- 关系
- 他们如何使用 Ads 账户

模板：

```text
Yes. [Agency/Partner Name] signs in to the Ads account to manage campaign setup, optimization, reporting, and account maintenance under our agreement.
```

### 24）Are any other relationships required to provide the branding used in ads or shown on the website?

如果广告或网站里出现了：
- 品牌商名称
- 授权品牌
- 经销品牌
- 加盟品牌
- 联名品牌

通常应如实填 `Yes`。

模板：

```text
Yes. We use the [Brand Name] branding based on our authorized relationship with [Brand Owner Name].
```

若没有品牌授权、加盟、经销、特许等关系，则填 `No`。

### 25）Who pays for the Google Ads account?

与前文保持一致，不要前后矛盾。

模板：

```text
The Google Ads account is paid for by [Legal Company Name / Full Legal Name].
```

### 26）Licenses or certifications

如果行业属于监管行业，例如：
- 医疗
- 金融
- 教育认证
- 法律
- 租赁
- 政府相关服务
- 票务
- 加密/金融衍生类

必须如实填写。

模板：

```text
The required license/certification is [License Name], reference number [ID Number], held by [Company Name].
```

如果不属于受监管行业，按真实情况选择无需许可证。

### 27）How does your company protect personal information?

模板：

```text
We protect customer information through secure website access controls, limited internal access, and established privacy procedures. Our privacy policy is available at: https://example.com/privacy-policy
```

注意：

- 最好确保网站上确实有 `Privacy Policy`
- 同时建议有：
  - Terms / Terms of Service
  - Contact Us
  - About Us
  - Refund / Shipping / Return Policy（如适用）

### 28）Attach any additional documents that support the claims made above

建议上传：

- 营业执照 / 注册证明
- 授权书 / 合同 / 服务协议
- 付款主体证明（如有需要）
- 行业许可证
- 说明品牌关系、履约关系、代运营关系的文件

原则：

- 同一份文件能证明多个点时，不用重复上传多次

## Part 5 - Closing Questions

### 29）Other comments or information

可作为补充说明位，尤其适合在业务链较复杂时使用。

通用模板：

```text
We have provided information and supporting documents describing our business, website, payment responsibility, and any relevant business relationships involved in advertising and fulfillment. All information submitted is accurate to the best of our knowledge.

如果存在代理/授权/第三方履约，也可以补一句：

```

## 五、提交前最终核对清单

提交前务必逐项检查：

### 5.1 名称一致性

- [ ] Ads 账户使用主体与表单主体一致
- [ ] 营业执照名称与表单名称完全一致
- [ ] 付款主体与表单说明一致
- [ ] 授权品牌名称与授权文件一致

### 5.2 网站一致性

- [ ] 表单填写的网站就是广告实际使用的网站
- [ ] 网站可正常访问
- [ ] 网站内有隐私政策、联系方式、业务说明
- [ ] 网站内提到的第三方关系都能解释清楚

### 5.3 关系链一致性

- [ ] 谁创建广告内容已写清楚
- [ ] 谁制作网站内容已写清楚
- [ ] 谁登录 Ads 账户已写清楚
- [ ] 谁提供产品/服务已写清楚
- [ ] 谁履约/谁负责售后已写清楚
- [ ] 是否存在代理、联盟、授权、代发货、经销关系已写清楚

### 5.4 材料合规性

- [ ] 上传的是 PDF/JPG/JPEG/PNG
- [ ] 不是网页截图冒充证件
- [ ] 文件清晰、可读
- [ ] 可遮挡隐私，但关键信息清楚
- [ ] 材料能证明你在表单里写的内容

## 六、四种常见业务模式的简版参考答案

### 6.1 自营电商

```text
We are an e-commerce business selling [product category] directly through our website. Our in-house team manages the website and ad content. Customers click our ads, visit our website, place orders online, and receive the products through our fulfillment process. Our company is responsible for fulfillment and customer support.
```

### 6.2 代理代投

```text
We advertise the products/services of [Client Name]. Our company / agency manages Google Ads campaigns under a formal service agreement. The advertised business is [Client Name], and supporting documents are provided to explain the relationship.
```

### 6.3 联盟营销 / Affiliate

```text
We operate as an affiliate marketing business. We promote offers from partner merchants and direct users to those offers through our website and advertising channels. The merchant or partner ultimately provides the goods/services, and supporting documents explain the relationship where applicable.
```

### 6.4 品牌授权 / 经销

```text
We market and sell products under [Brand Name] based on our authorized relationship with the brand owner. Our website and ads use the branding in accordance with this authorization, and supporting documentation is attached.
```

## 九、注意事项

- 这份模板是**辅助整理与代填框架**，不是让任何人照抄固定答案。
- Google 对关系链和主体真实性查得比较严，**不能编造事实**。
- 如果客户业务模式复杂，宁可解释得更清楚，也不要偷懒写成模糊答案。
- 如果网站上写了多个品牌或多个公司名字，但你表单里没解释，极容易被退回。
